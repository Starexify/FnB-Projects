The Item

Void Bottle

The Void Bottle is a unique material item that captures a fragment of the void from End portals and gateways. It serves as a key ingredient in the mod's crafting system and carries a powerful (and dangerous) secondary use.

Obtaining

Approach an End Portal or End Gateway and right-click with a Glass Bottle. This action will allow you to collect a little bit of void, transforming your glass bottle into a Void Bottle - your very own pocket-sized black hole!

How to obtain

Right-clicking an End Portal / Gateway with a Glass Bottle

Usages

1) Crafting Transport Modules

As a key ingredient, the Void Bottle unlocks the potential to craft a variety of transport modules. These modules will be essential for your logistical endeavors within the mod, allowing you to move items, resources, and even yourself across the map with ease.

2) Embrace the Void (Drinking it)

If you're feeling bold and your survival instincts are waning, the Void Bottle offers a final, albeit permanent, solution. Drinking the void within the bottle will instantly end your journey in the game - even in Creative mode.

This is a risky move, especially in Hardcore mode, where death is final. Think carefully before taking this step, as it will erase your progress and leave you with a blank slate.

⚠️ Warning: If you don't have keepInventory true, all items from your inventory will be permanently deleted upon death from drinking the Void Bottle.
